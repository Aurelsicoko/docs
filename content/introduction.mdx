The **Content** tab is your built-in CMS. Every piece of text, image, and link generated by Fimo is editable and stored here, making it easy to collaborate and manage content at scale.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Built-in CMS" icon="database">
    All site content is editable by default.  
    No setup needed, everything lives in the Content tab.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Real-time updates" icon="bolt">
    Changes appear instantly across teammates’ preview windows.  
    Edit once, see it everywhere.
  </Card>

  <Card title="AI credits saved" icon="coins">
    Fix typos or make small changes directly in the CMS without using AI credits.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Scalable management" icon="arrows-split-up-and-left">
    Prepare for advanced workflows like translation, A/B testing, and content experiments.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Why it matters

<Tip>
The Content tab combines the flexibility of a CMS with Fimo’s real-time infrastructure.  
If User A edits content, User B instantly sees the update in their preview window.
</Tip>

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Without a CMS" icon="ban">
    <ul>
      <li>Every edit goes through AI chat.</li>
      <li>Credits consumed for small fixes.</li>
      <li>No central hub for managing text or media.</li>
    </ul>
  </Card>

  <Card title="With Fimo CMS" icon="check">
    <ul>
      <li>All content stored and editable in one place.</li>
      <li>No credits needed for manual edits.</li>
      <li>Updates visible in real time across teammates.</li>
      <li>Ready for future features like A/B tests and translations.</li>
    </ul>
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Workflow at a glance

<Steps>
  <Step title="Generate your site with AI">
    Fimo creates pages with editable text, images, and links.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage content in the CMS">
    Update entries directly in the Content tab, fix typos, or make bulk edits.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Collaborate in real time">
    Teammates see changes instantly in their preview window, no refresh needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
