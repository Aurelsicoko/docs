The **Content** tab is your built-in CMS. Every piece of text, image, and link generated by Fimo is editable and stored here, making it easy to collaborate and manage content at scale.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Built-in CMS" icon="database">
    All site content is editable by default.  
    No setup needed, everything lives in the Content tab.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Real-time updates" icon="bolt">
    Changes appear instantly across teammates’ preview windows.  
    Edit once, see it everywhere.
  </Card>

  <Card title="AI credits saved" icon="hand-holding-dollar">
    Fix typos or make small changes directly in the CMS without using AI credits.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Scalable management" icon="arrows-split-up-and-left">
    Prepare for advanced workflows like translation, A/B testing, and content experiments.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Why it matters

<Callout icon="bolt" color="var(--primary)" iconType="solid">
  <strong>Real-time collaboration</strong> — If <strong>User A</strong> updates content, <strong>User B</strong> sees it instantly in their preview window. No refresh needed.
</Callout>
